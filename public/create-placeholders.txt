To complete the birthday website, please add these 3 image files to the public/ folder:

1. birthday-banner.png - Birthday banner (800x200px recommended)
   Download from: https://www.flaticon.com or search "happy birthday banner png"

2. birthday-cake.gif - Animated cake (300x300px recommended)  
   Download from: https://giphy.com/search/birthday-cake

3. balloon-border.png - Balloon decorations (full width, 200px height)
   Search: "balloon border png transparent"

After adding these files, open index-new.html in your browser!
